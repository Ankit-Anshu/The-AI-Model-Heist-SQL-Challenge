The Story

On the morning of November 15th, 2024, VortexAI discovered a major breach. Their revolutionary AI model, set to change the industry, was leaked. Internal security suggests an insider is behind the theft. Can you help Detective Ankit find the culprit?

Hint 2: Check what files were accessed during this time

Hint 3: Look specifically for access to the AI model file

Hint 4: Check for large data transfers

Hint 5: Analyze network traffic for suspicious activity
